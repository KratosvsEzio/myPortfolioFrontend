Welcome to Manhattan Prep's simulated GRE exam platform! 

There are a few things to keep in mind while taking your practice exam.

1. Wireless Users -  The computer needs to have a constant connection between the browser and the server.  If you have a weak wireless signal, either move closer to the signal source or plug the PC into the network.  If you lose your connection while data is being transmitted between questions, your exam could be invalidated.

2. Please DO NOT PRESS THE BACK BUTTON on the browser while taking the exam.  This will cause unexpected behavior that could invalidate your exam.
