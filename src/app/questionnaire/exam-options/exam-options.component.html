Customize Timing:

The default timing for an exam is ( Essay = 30 min each, Verbal = 30 min each, Math = 35 min each). Choosing alternate timing options will not accurately simulate a test-day experience and can result in a different score than you would receive using the default timing option.

Customize Delivery:

To take a modified version of the exam (e.g., a math-only exam, a verbal-only exam) change the settings here. The "Default" option will deliver a full GRE exam (two essays, two math sections, and two verbal sections).



The additional customized delivery options: (Math Only, Verbal Only, Essays Only, Essays and Math, Essays and Verbal,Math and Verbal) are not available for the Free GRE Practice Test.

IMPORTANT NOTE: Once these options are selected for this exam, they CANNOT be reset. Future practice exams will allow you to choose different accommodations.
